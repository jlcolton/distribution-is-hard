# Distribution Is Hard — Don't F*ck It Up

Most founders build something good and then wonder why nobody shows up.

This is the system for fixing it.

## What This Is

A distribution framework for founders — from finding people who already have the problem you solve, to getting them to pay attention, to converting that attention into traction.

This isn't marketing advice. It's an operating system built from real patterns: what actually works when you have no team, no budget, and no existing audience.

## Who This Is For

- Founders who built something and can't get anyone to care
- Solo operators who don't have a marketing team and don't want one
- Builders who know their product works but can't translate that into traction
- First GTM hires trying to figure out where to start
